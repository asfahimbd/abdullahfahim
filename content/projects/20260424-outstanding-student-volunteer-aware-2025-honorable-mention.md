I am truly humbled to receive the Outstanding Student Volunteer Award (Honourable Mention) from the IEEE Bangladesh Section for my contributions over the last year. It was a special privilege to have this recognition handed over by Professor Dr. M. Imamul Hassan Bhuiyan sir, the Honorable Chair of IEEE Bangladesh Section.

This award recognizes my dedication and commitment to IEEE, having served as the Chairperson of the IEEE JUST Student Branch during this period. It stands as a testament to the impact and growth I was able to bring to the branch.

I am deeply thankful to IEEE JUST SB, and all the members and advisors for guiding me and supporting me throughout this journey.
